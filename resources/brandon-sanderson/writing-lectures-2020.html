Brandon Sanderson on Writing Science Fiction and Fantasy

Background

In 2020, acclaimed fantasy author Brandon Sanderson delivered a series of lectures at Brigham Young University (BYU) that are available online. These lectures offer a deep dive into various aspects of writing, including worldbuilding, character development, plotting, and magic systems. Sanderson shares his expertise, providing aspiring writers with valuable insights and practical advice to enhance their craft. From constructing immersive fictional worlds to creating well-rounded characters and crafting intricate plots, Sanderson's lectures are a treasure trove of knowledge and inspiration for writers seeking to refine their skills and storytelling techniques.
